It was founded in 1985 as SRM Engineering College in Kattankulathur.

There are 5 different branches. They are:

  1. Kattankulathur Campus
  2. Ramapuram Campus
  3. Vadapalani Campus
  4. NCR Campus
  5. Amaravati Campus
